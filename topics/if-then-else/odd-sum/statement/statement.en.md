## Odd Sum
Given 3 numbers. Decide whether 2 of them can be chosen such that their sum is odd. If you can, print YES, if not, print NO.

### Input
The input has three integers in three rows: $A,B,C$.

### Output
If 2 of the three numbers can be selected so that their sum is odd, then print YES, if not, then print NO.

### Constraints
* $1 \le A,B,C \le 1000$

### Example 1 input
    10
    10
    10

### Example 1 output
    NO

### Explanation of the example 1
The sum of any two numbers is $10+10=20$ even, so the answer is NO.

### Example 2 Input
    10
    11
    12

### Example output 2
    YES

### Explanation of example 2
Since $10+11=21$ is odd, the answer is YES.
