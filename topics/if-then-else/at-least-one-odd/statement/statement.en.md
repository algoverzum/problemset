## At Least One Odd
Odd numbers are the ones that are not divisible by 2. I will give you two numbers. Can you tell me whether there is an odd number among them?

### Input
The input contains two positive integer numbers on separate lines: $A$ and $B$. 

### Output
Print 1 if at least one of the two numbers is odd, and -1 otherwise.

### Constraints
* $1 \le A, B \le 100$

### Example input
    13
    37

### Example output
    1

### Explanation of the example
13 and 37 are both odd and if both numbers are odd, we also need to print 1, because it is true that at least one of them is odd.
