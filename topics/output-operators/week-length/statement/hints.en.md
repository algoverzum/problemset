### Hint 1
Use multiplication to calculate the answer. Reminder: the sign of multiplication is `*`, you can print the result with `cout <<`, and a `;` is needed at the end of each instruction.

### Hint 2
A minute has 60 seconds, an hour has 60 * 60 seconds, a day has 24 times as much...

### Hint 3
    cout << 7 * 24 * 60 * 60;
