## Rope Cutting
At least we have the same metric system to measure length! We have a rope that has a length of 13m and 37cm, and we need to cut it to pieces of length 42cm (that is the ideal length for catching a wrookie). Can you write a program that tells how many pieces we can make and what will be the length of the last remaining part?

### Output
You need to print two integer numbers separated by a space. The first is the number of 42cm-length pieces that we can make, and the second is the length of the rope that remains afterwards, in cm.
