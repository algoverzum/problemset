## gcd recursively
In the depths of the galaxy, two violent races struggled for power. But even in the greatest war, there must be a common point, where opposites meet. During a secret Jedi mission, two distant systems in the galaxy, Alderaan and Tatooine, sent two numbers to each other. The Jedi Council decided to calculate the greatest common divisor (gcd) of these two numbers to find the connection between them.
The wise Yoda asks you to write a **recursive** function that determines the greatest common divisor of two positive numbers.

### Template
Start from the pre-made template code! Do not change anything in the main program, otherwise it will not be accepted. You must write the inside of the function `gcd`, which takes $A$ and $B$ as parameters.

### Constraints
* $1 \le A,B \le 10^6$

### Example
For example, the greatest common divisor of 13 and 21 is 1, since 13 is prime and 21 = 3*7. 
The greatest common divisor of 42 and 66 is 6, since 66=6*11 and 42=6*7.
