## Sum Recursive
In a distant galaxy, in the ancient sanctuary of the Jedi Order, there existed a riddle that symbolized the flow of the Force. The object of the puzzle was to determine the sum of all the numbers $a$ and $b$. This calculation reflected the continuity and harmony of the Force, each step linked to the previous one, just like the threads of the Force.
The Jedi Masters ask you to solve the problem using **recursion**.

### Template
Start from the pre-made template code! Do not change anything in the main program, otherwise it will not be accepted. You need to write the inside of the `summa` function, which takes $a$ and $b$ as parameters.

### Constraints
* $1 \le a, b \le 10^5$

### Example
If $a=3$ and $b=6$, the sum of $3+4+5+6=18$.
