## Names List
The crew roster on your spaceship was not kept up to date, the names are in the order they came on board and the system is becoming unintuitive to you. Arrange the roster in alphabetical ascending order.

### Input
The first line of the input is a single integer: $N$ is the crew size
The second line of the input contains $N$ words $S$ separated by spaces: the names of the crew

### Output
Print $N$ strings, the names of the crew alphabetically

### Constraints
$1 \le N \le 1000$<br>
The $S$ words contain lowercase letters of the english alphabet, and are atmost 1000 characters long.

### Example input
    6
    luke leia han chewbacca kenobi vader

### Example output
    chewbacca han kenobi leia luke vader 

### Explanation of the example
c<h<k<le<lu<v
