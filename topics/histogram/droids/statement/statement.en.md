## Droids
In a droid factory, 10 different types of droids are produced, numbered from 1 to 10. You have received a list containing the types of all manufactured droids. Your task is to determine how many times the most common droid type appears.

### Input
The first line of the input contains $N$ - the length of the list
The second line of the input contains $N$ numbers between 1 and 10, separated by spaces, the types of the manufactured droids.

### Output
Print a single number: the amount of times the most common droid type appears.

### Constraints
* $1 \le N \le 1\,000\,000$

### Example input
    10
    1 2 4 1 3 1 1 4 2 1

### Example output
    5

### Explanation of the example
5 of type 1 droids were produced. (The droid type was not a question.)
