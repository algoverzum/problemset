## Total petals
Aladar is picking flowers. He sees $N$ flowers in the meadow, he knows how many petals each one has (these numbers are listed in the input). He only picks the flowers with odd petals. At home, he removes the petals from the flowers. How many petals will he have?

### Input
The first line of the input contains the number of flowers: $N$. Then $N$ integers are given on separate lines, the number of petals of each flower ($S_1, S_2, \ldots, S_N$).  

### Output
You must print one number, the number of petals of all the flowers that Aladar will take home. 

### Constraints
* $1 \le N \le 100$
* $1 \le S_i \le 100$

### Example input
    5
    7
    4
    3
    11
    6

### Example output
    21

### Explanation of the example
There are 5 flowers in the meadow. Aladar picks the flowers with 7, 3 and 11 petals.
