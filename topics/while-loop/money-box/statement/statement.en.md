## Money Box
My friend Tamara collects money in her wrookie-box, which is initially empty. On the first day, she puts 1 coin in it, on the second day 2 coins, and so on, every day one more than the previous day. She wants to know, after how many days will she have at least $N$ coins in the box?

### Input
The first line of the input contains $N$ - the desired number of coins. 

### Output
Print the smallest integer $K$ for which it is true that after $K$ days she will have at least $N$ coins.

### Constraints
* $1 \le N \le 10\,000$

### Example input
    22

### Example output
    7

### Explanation of the example
After 7 days, Tamara will have $1+2+3+4+5+6+7=28$ coins in her wrookie-box, and that is enough. After 6 days, she will have only 21 coins, which is not enough yet.
