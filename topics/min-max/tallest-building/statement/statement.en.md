## Tallest Building
Welcome to our planet! In our capital, Dupabest, there are $N$ buildings whose heights are $H_1, H_2, \ldots, H_N$, given in inch, integers. Currently, the tallest building is the control tower of the spaceport, and it is forbidden to build anything higher than this. Find out what this maximum height is.

### Input
The first line of the input contains $N$ - the the number of buildings. In the second row, $N$ integers are the heights of the buildings ($H_1, H_2, \ldots, H_N$).

### Output
Print a single number, the height of the tallest building.

### Constraints
* $1 \le N \le 100$
* $1 \le H_i \le 100\,000$

### Example input
    5
    888 234 900 1337 42

### Example output
    1337

### Explanation of the example
The highest of the heights is 1337.
