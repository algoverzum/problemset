## Swap Min Max
In a classroom with $N$ students. The teacher plays a game with his students where there is a pile of candy on the teacher's desk, each student can take as much as they want, but the student who takes the most candy has to swap with the student who took the least.

### Input
The first line of the input is the number of students $N$.
The second line in the input is the number of candies of the students. $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_{N}$.

### Output
You need to print the number of candies of all students on one line, separated by spaces.

### Limits
* $1 \le N \le 1000$
* $1 \le A \le 1000$

### Example input
    5
    3 4 5 2 1

### Example output
    3 4 1 2 5

### Explanation of the example
    The third student took the most candies, five. 
    The fifth student took the least, one.
